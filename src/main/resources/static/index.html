The 5 Gallon Bucket

EST 1967

A fine example of a modern 5 gallon bucket.

Background

"In 1967, William Roper, whose family owned a plastic-molding business in Los Angeles, revolutionized packaging with the first five-gallon plastic pail and lid. Today, in nondescript industrial parks across North America-in places like Elk Grove Village, Ill., Edison, N.J., Valpariso, Ind., and Fullerton, Calif. U.S. and Canadian companies make an estimated 170 million five-gallon pails each year." Arndt, M (1994, October 16). SAFETY PANEL POURS ITSELF INTO 5-GALLON BUCKET FUROR. Chicago Tribune.
